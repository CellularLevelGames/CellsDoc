14 General stuff:
15 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
16 
17 * Battle start: the moment enemies are detected nearby a unit. Specific for each unit.
18 * Battle end: the moment no enemies are any more nearby, also individually tracked per unit.
19 * 'Any' state: To make the SM more readable with order able to interrupt any other state, the 'any' state
20  represents the state the SM is currently in. Transitions from 'any' state are listed directly with the
21  SM component and are always valid.
22 
23 All Unit AI states are based on the **mechanics::UnitAiBaseState**, which offers each state to define
24 possible interrupts, immediately triggering transitions.
25 This can e.g. be used to abort a move state when taking damage.
26 
27 Idle Behaviour implementation \anchor unit_ai_idle_impl
28 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
29 
30 As shown above, the \ref unit_ai_idle "requirements" are realized by three states.
31 
32 The mechanics::IdleState and mechanics::AutoAttackState rely on the mechanics::AttackTargetTracker to raise
33 events for battle start and end, as well as target switching during battle.
34 
35 The mechanics::AutoGatherState state then implements a method for food selection, according to the
36 \ref unit_ai_gather "Gather behaviour" requirements.
37 AutoGather is only transitionary (one frame) and has thus no interrupt sources.
38 After food selection, the gather behaviour is entered.
39 
40 Importantly, after food selection a unit already reserves a slot to carry that food. The intention is to have
41 a clear distribution of carriers among food items immediately after a battle.
42 See mechanics::GatherTargetSelector.
43 
44 Gather Behaviour implementation \anchor unit_ai_gather_impl
45 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
46 
47 Link to \ref unit_ai_gather "requirements".
48 A food item has already been selected, a carry slot on the food reserved for the unit and the food been
49 set as movement target, prior to this behaviour during mechanics::AutoGatherState or when the GatherOrder
50 was given, as interpreted by the UnitAiStateMachine.
51 
52 The following mechanics::MoveState is duplicated: one for interrupt by close enemies and one for interrupt
53 only by taking damage (for the explicit gather order by the player).
54 
55 Once the food is reached, the mechanics::DeliverState takes over, see details there.
