Multiplayer

Startup and Menus

For simplicity, we for now assume an always-on implementation. Upon start, the user is informed of the login process. Each user automatically creates a room with his profile, so his friends will be able to join already.

Alternatively, he can decide to join himself, which will make him close his own room and join a selected friend's room.
