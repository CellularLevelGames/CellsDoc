3 Unit AI
4 =======
5 
6 This page describes how general units are designed to behave.
7 An overview of the resulting AI state machine is shown in the implementation description \ref page_unit_ai_impl.
8 Each behaviour can be made up of multiple states traversed to realize the requirements here.
9 
10 Default behaviour
11 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
12 
13 Newly created units shall:
14 
15 * If there is a gather point, do an Attack Move there (see below).
16 * If there is no gather point, Idle.
17 The Idle behaviour is also the default after most actions.
18 
19 Idle \anchor unit_ai_idle
20 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
21 
22 * Checks for opponents in a certain guard range when this state is entered.
23 * If there are none, does idles until an opponent enters the guard range.
24 * According to the target selection described below, one opponent should be the current target.
25 * Once in melee range, the current target is attacked.
26 * When all opponents are defeated the gathering behaviour is activated with a selected food item.
27 * If no food is found the unit shall idle.
28 * If food is found, the most appropriate is selected and the Gather Behaviour entered.
29 
30 Food selection should be done with the following selection rule priority, from top to bottom:
31 
32 1. Eligible food are the items that are in the unit's guard range and can be carried.
33 2. Helping other units muster the required strength to carry a food they are waiting at.<br>
34  -> Food with missing strength, and the lowest value among these.
35 3. Food with the highest value.
36 4. The closest item.
37 
38 For implementation, see \ref unit_ai_idle_impl "Idle Behaviour Implementation".
39 
40 Gather \anchor unit_ai_gather
41 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
42 
43 Gathering shall be activated:
44 
45 * either by an explicit order to gather a specific item,
46 * or when a battle has ended and was not part of another command preventing gathering.
47  (Example: AttackMove units will continue to their destination after a fight and not gather)
48 
49 While a unit moves to pick up an item it shall:
50 
51 * Only be interrupted by taking damage if the gathering was a specific order.
52 * First fight nearby enemies if the gathering was automatic after a battle.
53 
54 For implementation, see \ref unit_ai_gather_impl "Gather Behaviour Implementation".
55 
56 Move \anchor unit_ai_move
57 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
58 
59 The unit shall move to a designated point. No interrupts shall be possible, so the unit shall continue even if
60 taking damage.
61 For implementation, see \ref unit_ai_move_impl "Move Behaviour Implementation".
62 
63 Ordered Attack \anchor unit_ai_attack
64 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
65 
66 While during and automatic attack the target may be switched, an ordered attack by the user shall not be
67 interrupted, except by the target having been defeated or being out of range.

74 Explicit Guard
75 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
76 
77 As Guard above, but with the following changes:
78 
79 * The unit has to return to the original guard position when all targets are lost. The original position is
80  where guard mode was first entered.
81 * Returning to its original position, a unit with explicit guard does not pick up food after battle.
