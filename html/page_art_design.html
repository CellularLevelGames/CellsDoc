Art Design

2D versus 3D

Using 2D or 3D has implications on many art and also game design aspects. In both cases we will be limited to simple styles, as we currently have no artists on the team :)

  • Rendering Pipeline useable
  • Creation of Artwork: Painting Sprites vs. 3D Modelling
  • Possible camera perspectives & immersion
  • Making of Animations

Pro 3D

  • Simple 3D Models are quickly made and can look very good and clean if done right
  • Camera movement to first person or very close views allows for more immersion (in-the-middle feeling)
  • We currently have more experience in 3D due to BomberUnits being in 3D
  • Animations can be done quite simply by moving and saving model parts

Pro 2D

  • Simple level design by using tilemaps
  • Inherently grid-based, making many other things easier
  • Art design can be easily changed and improved later, as the actual sprite does not have big influence on game mechanics

Decision

We decided to go for a 2D settings for now. If things go bad, we can probably still move the game to a 3D world.

Organic microbiology theme

This theme is not used widely in games currently and offers the benefit of simple shapes or drawings and high artistic freedom for surroundings, characters, effects etc.
